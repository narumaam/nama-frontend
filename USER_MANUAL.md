# NAMA: Networked Autonomous Marketplace Architecture
## User Manual & Admin Guide (v3.0)

Welcome to NAMA. This guide explains how to manage your travel agency operations on the platform.

### 1. Account & Organization Settings
#### 1.1 How to Change Your Base Currency
As a DMC or Travel Agency Admin (Level 3), you can set your preferred currency for all quotations and financial logs.
1.  Navigate to **Dashboard > Settings > Organization**.
2.  Locate the **Base Currency** dropdown.
3.  Select between **INR, USD, AED, or EUR**. 
4.  *Note:* Changing this will update the dynamic pricing engine to fetch exchange rates for all new itineraries.

#### 1.2 Managing Your Team (L4 Users)
1.  Go to **Settings > Team**.
2.  Click **Invite User** and enter their email.
3.  Assign a Role (e.g., Sales Manager, Ops Executive, Finance).

---

### 2. Operating the AI Agents
#### 2.1 Generating an Itinerary
1.  Go to **Leads**. Click on a lead captured from WhatsApp.
2.  Click **"Generate AI Itinerary"**.
3.  Select the **Style** (Budget, Standard, Luxury).
4.  The system will assemble "Bento Blocks" in < 2 minutes. You can drag and drop to reorder these.

#### 2.2 Autonomous Bidding
1.  Inside an itinerary, click on any **Hotel** or **Transfer** block.
2.  Select **"Start Broadcast"**.
3.  Choose your preferred suppliers and click **"Begin AI Negotiation"**.
4.  The AI will negotiate net rates and alert you once a bid matches your target price.

---

### 3. White-Label Portals
To give your sub-agents or customers a branded experience:
1.  Navigate to **Portals**.
2.  Upload your **Logo** and choose your **Brand Colors**.
3.  If you have a custom domain (e.g., `portal.travelagency.com`), enter it in the **CNAME** field.

---

### 4. Support & Feedback
NAMA is built to evolve. If you need a design change or a new feature based on client feedback:
1.  Log it in the **Feedback Module**.
2.  Your development team can adjust the **Next.js** components or **Tailwind** styles to match your new requirements.
